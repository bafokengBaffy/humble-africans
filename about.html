Our Story

Humble Africans is a clothing brand that brings Africa together by celebrating different cultures on hoodies, T-shirts, caps and more. Our first collection shows Lesotho, South Africa, Botswana and Eswatini cultural patterns.

Founded in 2025 by Bafokeng Jeremiah Khoali, Humble Africans was born from a desire to showcase African unity while creating employment opportunities for young Basotho.

Our Mission

We want to give young people work making and selling clothes while teaching design and marketing skills. By telling our story on each garment, we want to build pride in Lesotho and connect with friends across Africa.

Celebrate Culture

Showcase authentic African patterns and designs that represent our diverse heritage.

Empower Youth

Provide employment and skills training in design, sewing, and business for young Africans.

Promote Unity

Connect people across Africa through shared appreciation of our cultural heritage.

Our Objectives

Year 1 Goals

  • Make and sell our first 100 pieces in Lesotho
  • Hire and train 5 Basotho youth in design, sewing and sales
  • Open a simple online store and two small stalls in Lesotho
  • Establish partnerships with local fabric suppliers

Years 2-3 Goals

  • Add collections for South Africa, Botswana and Eswatini
  • Grow team to 20 youth across all four countries
  • Open more stalls in nearby different countries
  • Develop school uniform program with cultural designs
  • Launch apprenticeship program for fashion design
