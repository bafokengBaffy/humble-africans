Our Story

Humble Africans is a clothing brand that brings Africa together by celebrating different cultures on hoodies, T-shirts, caps and more. Our first collection shows Lesotho, South Africa, Botswana and Eswatini cultural patterns.

We want to give young people work making and selling clothes while teaching design and marketing skills. By telling our story on each garment, we want to build pride in Lesotho and connect with friends across Africa.
